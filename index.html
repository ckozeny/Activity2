About Me

Hello! My name is Cj Kozeny. I'm an MIS major here at UNO and this year I am a senior. I've always had an interest in the information sciences and a nagging curiosity of how things work and operate. I get satisfaction from taking things apart and learning about their inner workings - maybe this drive is what influenced me to pursue this type of career. In my free time i love to be with friends and family, snowboard when the weather permits, and be out in the sun as much as possible.

Education

My education in these topics has mostly been provided by the university. I've also learned a good chunk of my skills through curiosity and trial and error. I started with modifying Xbox controllers for my friends to shoot at rapid speeds, onto installing car audio and amplifiers, and finally onto system development and database management. I find pride in expanding my knowledge every day, and hope to continue that with this course.

Recreation

I choose to spend my free time doing a lot of different activities. Most of them involve being active or working with problems and finding viable solutions. I like to work on cars and ride ro drive anything with a motor. I have a cabin that i frequent almost every summer weekend, and spend a majority of my time there. I'll try anything once!
